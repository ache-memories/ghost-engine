Frequently Asked Questions

What makes SEOGhostEngine different?

It is built for speed. While other tools load hundreds of scripts, our engine focus on the essential technical data delivered in a terminal-like environment.

Why do some sites fail to load?

Modern browsers have strict security (CORS). We use a cloud proxy to bypass this, but highly protected sites may still block automated requests.

Is it secure and private?

Absolutely. We don't track your search history or store data. SEOGhostEngine is a client-side tool designed for privacy-conscious developers.
